About me

Starting a career in software development is not easy peasy lemon squeezy, but rather difficult difficult, lemon difficult. This was a life changing realization for me. Altho some might call it a setback, I call it a challenge, and there is nothing more compelling and motivating than accomplishing a hard to achieve goal. Fast forward 4 years, and I am having the time of my life, developing end-to-end solutions, maintaining and improving products and teaching others how to code and start their own careers
